## Rules

- Personalize every message with a real reason the partnership fits.
- Do not buy bulk links, use automated directory blasts, or trade irrelevant links.
- Do not claim awards, fleet ownership, service guarantees, or customer results without evidence.
- Keep the business name, phone, domain, service area, and email consistent everywhere.
- Track contact name, site, date, response, next action, and resulting link in one outreach sheet.
- Request review approval before publishing any co-branded copy or logo.
