Good to know

Before you request.

Are the online rates final?

They are starting daily rates. Your final quote reflects the exact date, rental length, mileage, delivery, deposit, and available vehicle.

How does delivery work?

KD's Exotics is delivery-only. After approval, the rental desk confirms an eligible handoff location and arrival window with you.

When is the reservation confirmed?

Your reservation is confirmed only after the vehicle and driver are approved, documents are reviewed, and the agreement, deposit, and payment arrangements are complete.

Can I speak with someone first?

Yes. Call the private rental desk at (213) 264-2967 for help choosing a vehicle or planning delivery.
