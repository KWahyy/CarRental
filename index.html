Private Rental Desk · FAQ

Before you arrive.

Straight answers on approval, insurance, delivery, pricing, and the details that shape your reservation.

Need a private answer? Call the rental desk
01What do I need to rent an exotic car?

You will need a valid driver's license, proof of active auto insurance, and an approved security deposit. Vehicle-specific eligibility requirements are reviewed before your reservation is confirmed.

02How does vehicle delivery work?

KD's Exotics is a delivery-only service with no customer-facing storefront. We confirm an eligible Southern California delivery location, arrival window, and any delivery charge with you before booking.

03Do I need my own auto insurance?

Proof of active auto insurance is required for approval. Enter your insurance company in the quote form, and we will confirm whether your coverage meets the requirements for the vehicle you requested.

04How quickly will I receive a quote?

After you submit the vehicle, rental date, and contact information, we check current availability and follow up with pricing and approval steps. Response time can vary with demand and vehicle availability.

05Are the prices shown online the final rental price?

Online prices are starting daily rates. Your final quote may change based on the vehicle, date, rental length, mileage allowance, delivery location, deposit, and selected add-ons.

06Can I add a photographer to my rental?

Yes. Select Photographer under Add-ons when requesting a quote. We will confirm photographer availability, timing, package details, and pricing before your reservation is finalized.
